About


ENIAC STUDENT FEED is a web-based student feedback system designed to efficiently collect, analyze, and manage course-related feedback. It enables students to provide valuable insights on subject course outcomes, fostering continuous improvement in the learning experience. Faculty members can access feedback to refine their teaching strategies, while the admin (HOD) has comprehensive oversight of all feedback data, facilitating informed academic decision-making.

Features


Anonymous Feedback Option – Allow students to submit feedback anonymously to ensure honest responses

Feedback Categorization – Organize feedback based on different parameters like subject, semester, or faculty.

User Authentication – Secure login for students, faculty, and admin.

Data Analytics & Reports – Visual representation of feedback trends for better decision-making.

Export & Download Reports – Allow faculty/admins to download feedback reports for offline analysis.
